medication
Recommended Treatment
expand_more

Immediate action is required to prevent spread.

Organic Option: Apply copper-based fungicides or bio-fungicides containing Bacillus subtilis. Remove lower infected leaves to improve airflow.
Chemical Option: Use fungicides containing Chlorothalonil or Mancozeb every 7-10 days.
shield
Prevention Tips
expand_more
  • Rotate crops every 2-3 years away from Solanaceous family plants.
  • Use drip irrigation instead of overhead watering to keep foliage dry.
  • Apply mulch to prevent soil spores from splashing onto leaves.
  • Purchase disease-free seeds and resistant varieties.
